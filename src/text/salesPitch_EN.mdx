# Arsenal - The Swiss App for Gun Collectors

Arsenal is **the firearm inventory and gun collection manager** for serious collectors, made in Switzerland.
Track your guns, ammo stock, and accessories - **fully offline, one-time purchase (only 5 CHF), no subscription**.

Create a **collection of your weapons**, with photos and many parameters, including:
- Up to 5 photos with gallery mode
- Caliber (supports multiple per gun, e.g. .357 Magnum, .38 Special) with easy to use selection list
- Acquisition date with calendar selection
- Color with color selection
- Features for weapons according to Swiss Weapons Act Article 5
- Free comments
You can also add individual keywords!

Manage your **ammunition stock**, too:
- Last quantity change with calendar selection or automatically with QuickShot/QuickStock
- Critical quantity
- Free comments

Keep your **accessories and weapon parts** under control, too:
- Multiple subcategories of accessories and weapon parts such as silencers, barrels, optics, etc
- Each subcategory has its own parameters
- Link an accessory to another accessory, a weapon part or a gun, with visual depictions in the collection overview!
- Link a weapon part to another weapon part or a gun, with visual depictions in the collection overview!
- Quickly link something to something else or unlink it comfortably with a quick action

Don't forget your **collection of specialized literature**!

Create **PDF files** at the touch of a button:
- The complete weapons and/or ammunition collection as a tabular overview
- The weapons collection in accordance with Article 5 as a tabular overview
- An individual collection item presented nicely (e.g. for the club notice board)

**QuickShot**:\
Have you fired a weapon? With one click from the gun collection you can adjust the shot load - the compatible ammunition types from the ammunition stock are automatically displayed, so the ammunition stock of the selected caliber is automatically adjusted when the shot load is increased. Of course, the shot load can also be adjusted without affecting the stock.

**QuickStock**:\
Has your ammunition stock changed? With one click from the ammunition overview, you can adjust the stock, whether increasing or decreasing.

**QuickMount**:\
Quickly link an accessory or a weapon part to something else without having to edit the entry directly

**Stock warning**:\
If you have specified a critical quantity for a type of ammunition, the ammunition overview will give you a visual warning if your stock falls below this quantity.

**Cleaning reminder**:\
Define in what intervals you want to be reminded to clean something - choose from predefined time intervals or after X shots fired.

**Save and import** your databases - as a complete ZIP-file with database and images or separately for each collection in CSV-format (without images)! Import **your custom Excel files**, too (CSV)!

**Other functionality**:\
Tag filter, search function, multiple color schemes (light and dark), sorting functions, autocomplete and collection display as grid, list or compact list.

Everything is **100% offline** and requires no internet access. All data is **stored locally on the device** and can be exported if required. No servers, no telemetry (apart from the Playstore itself).

The app is available in **five languages**: German, French, Italian, English and... Rumantsch Grischun!

It was developed in Switzerland and is aimed at the needs of Swiss gun collectors, but can of course be used all over the world.
