# Arsenal - The Swiss App for Gun Collectors

Arsenal is **the firearm inventory and gun collection manager** for serious collectors, made in Switzerland.\
Track your guns, ammo stock, and accessories - **fully offline, one-time purchase (only 5 CHF), no subscription**.

Create a wide variety of **collections**, with photos and many parameters:
- Firearms
- Ammunition
- Suppressors
- Optics
- Scopes
- Lights & Lasers
- Magazines
- Miscellaneous Accessories
- Conversion Kits
- Barrels
- Books
- and new collections are added continuously!

Use **smart tools** that make everyday life as a firearms collector and shooter easier:
- QuickShot for quickly logging round counts, linked to your ammunition inventory
- QuickStock for easy ammunition inventory management
- QuickMount to conveniently assign accessories and parts to a firearm or another accessory or part

**PDF exports** are easy to generate too. Choose between predefined lists or **create your own** with exactly the attributes you need - for example for insurance purposes.\
CSV **export/import** is also integrated, as well as full database backup as a ZIP file including all images.

Already using **QR codes** on your firearms? Integrate them seamlessly into the app and scan directly to the desired item.\
Not using QR labels yet? **Create them right inside the app** - using a wide range of predefined label formats (Avery) or define your own.

There are **many additional features** such as stock warnings, cleaning interval reminders, keyword filters, search and sort functions, autocomplete, multiple views and color themes (light and dark), freely selectable units of measurement and much more.

Everything is **100% offline** - no internet connection required.\
All data is stored **locally on your device** and can be exported at any time. No servers, no telemetry.

The app is available in **five languages**: German, French, Italian, English and... Rumantsch Grischun!

Developed in Switzerland, tailored to the needs of Swiss firearms collectors - but of course usable worldwide.\
**One-time purchase, use forever**, updates for as long as the app exists. **No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads!**
